Featured Tools

Featuring the FlexiShot™ and Gyroflex™ Navigator, our tools offer advanced, modular solutions for precise wellbore data acquisition and versatile gyro service applications.

Gyroflex™

The Gyroflex™ Navigator is a versatile, modular north-seeking rate gyro instrument that supports multiple gyro service applications, including surface readout and continuous surveys.

Gyro-Flexibility

The Gyroflex™ Navigator instrument offers unparalleled “gyro-flexibility.”

Versatile Operations

The design allows for versatile operations, accommodating various gyro service applications.

Multiple Modes

The tool can be utilized in surface readout, memory, drop, and continuous survey modes.

Advanced Technology

This cutting-edge, field-tested north-seeking rate gyro instrument features a modular design.

FlexiShot™

The FlexiShot™ is a modular electronic surveying instrument designed for precise wellbore data acquisition in both Single-Shot and Multishot modes.

High-Precision Instrument

The FlexiShot™ is a high-precision electronic surveying instrument designed for wellbore data acquisition.

Modular Design

Its modular design supports both Single-Shot (SLX) and Multishot (EMS) modes.

Compatibility

The instrument is compatible with any conventional non-magnetic running gear, including PeeWee.

Versatile Application

This flexibility enables it to be used for a wide range of surveys and orientations.
